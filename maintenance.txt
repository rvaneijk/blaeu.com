 ===== Proud to be FREE from analytics and tracking cookies =====

This site is free from analytics software. AWSTATS has been disabled and the logfiles have been deleted. Your IP address is not collected for analytics purposes.

This site is free from tracking elements and does not place 3rd party cookies. The site runs on the Dokuwiki content management system. Few functional cookies are places. See also [[https://www.dokuwiki.org/faq:cookies|Cookies]].

DW<md5-hash>

Used for authentication after login. This holds the necessary data to (re)login a previously authenticated user.

    Importance: necessary for anyone who needs to log in
    Typical content: encrypted username and password
    Expires: At end of session

bb2_screener

Used for monitoring of access tries that where blocked by the badbehaviour plugin in the last days. 

    Importance: necessary
    Typical content: timestamp + ip address
    Expires: At end of session

Example badbehaviour report:

    Percent	Count	Reason
    75.47%	40	Required header 'Accept' missing
    20.75%	11	Web browser attempted to send a trackback
     1.89%	 1	User-Agent claimed to be Googlebot, claim appears to be false.
     1.89%	 1	User-Agent claimed to be MSIE, with invalid Windows version

DOKU_PREFS

Used for remembering helpful user preferences, like the size of the editor textarea.

    Importance: functional, after login
    Typical content: name/value pairs in plain text
    Expires: in a year

DokuWiki

The standard PHP session identifier. Used to hold temporary data and to avoid CSRF attacks.

    Importance: necessary
    Typical content: random ID
    Expires: At end of session
